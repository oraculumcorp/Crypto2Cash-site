What We Do

Crypto2Cash.io is a B2B matching platform. We take businesses with recurring crypto-to-fiat conversion needs and match them with licensed, regulated providers who can execute at competitive rates.

Businesses submit their requirements — volume, asset type, destination country, payout method — and we deliver competitive quotes from up to five licensed providers within 24 hours. Businesses pay nothing. Providers pay us a referral fee when they win the business.

We have zero interest in steering you toward any single provider. Our model only works if businesses get genuinely better deals through us — so we compete entirely on quality of matches.

Our Provider Standard

Licensing Verification

Every provider in our network holds a valid Money Transmitter License or equivalent regulation in their primary operating jurisdiction. We verify licensing status quarterly — providers with lapses are suspended.

Volume Capability

We only work with providers capable of handling the volume ranges our business clients operate at. Consumer-only platforms are excluded from our B2B network.

Compliance Track Record

Providers with unresolved regulatory actions or pending enforcement proceedings are excluded until matters are resolved. We run a quarterly compliance check on every provider in our network.

How We Make Money

Provider Referral Fees

When we match a business client to a provider and that provider wins the relationship, the provider pays us a referral fee. This fee comes from the provider's margin — it does not affect the rate quoted to the business.

Lead Type Pricing

Providers pay different referral fees based on lead quality. Basic leads (email + volume range) command lower fees. Qualified leads (volume verified, KYB pre-screened) command higher fees. Exclusive leads (single-provider delivery) command premium fees. Business clients always receive quotes at the same rate regardless of lead type.

No Business Client Fees

Business clients are never charged. Our incentive is to deliver the best match — that's what keeps providers paying us and businesses returning.
