Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026  ·  GDPR & CCPA Compliant  ·  Version 1.0

Short version: We collect your email when you subscribe, and anonymous analytics when you browse. We do not sell your data. Ever. You can delete everything by emailing contact@crypto2cash.io.

1. Who We Are

Crypto2Cash.io operates a B2B lead matching platform for crypto-to-fiat liquidity. We are the data controller for personal data processed in connection with this website. Contact: contact@crypto2cash.io

We do not collect: government IDs, financial account numbers, credit cards, SSNs, or identity documents. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery: Sending B2B market updates you subscribed to
  • Platform improvement: Analyzing usage patterns anonymously
  • Affiliate tracking: Attributing referral commissions using anonymous session IDs only
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable laws and regulations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Email newsletter subscriptions
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Analytics, security, affiliate tracking
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where required by applicable law
